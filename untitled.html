BIOGRAPHY

Pastor Eliphew Temitope Olajide (B.sc) The CEO and Founder of November Eighteen Technology and Investment company limited(Electrical & Mechanical Engineering) and a respected man of God in The Redeemed Christian Church of God (THE KING'S COURT).was born on the 18th of november 1980, in okiti pupa, ondo state got married to his beloved wife Olubukola Eliphew in 2008, with two lovely children. Being a Man sent from God and a unique minister of the gospel of Jesus Christ, his Spirit-filled and anointed leadership continues to propel a dynamic, multifaceted and global ministry.
Blessed with rare gifts of administration, planning, hospitality, counseling, mentoring, ministering comfort, care, and encouragement to many, bringing hope, love and succor to the hopeless. Endowed with immense initiative, and great capacity for hard work. A missionary and minister of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
A powerful intercessor, A pioneer of multiple projects, ministries and missions to the glory of God.
